Hey I'm Charity!

I'm a professional editor with a degree in editing and publishing from BYU. I grew up in Kansas but have been in Utah for the last several years. I love editing because it combines three of my favorite things: (1) working with people, (2) fixing things, and (3) reading! As crazy as it sounds, I really do love the minutiae of grammar and syntax. When I'm not reading for work, I love perusing a good C.S. Lewis novel, thrifting, and trying to my hardest learn Spanish. I've had experience in just about every type of editing, from developing stories and filling plot holes, to fixing commas and periods. That's a little bit of my story; I can't wait to hear yours!
